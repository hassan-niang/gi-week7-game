How to Play

Choose from the following weapons: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.

The Rules:

  • Rock beats scissors (Rock crushes scissors)
  • Scissors beat paper (Scissors cut paper)
  • Paper beats rock (Paper covers rock)

How to Win?

The winner is determined by the rules mentioned above. For example, if one player chooses rock and the other chooses scissors, the player with rock wins.
Ties: If both players choose the same shape, the game is a tie, and it's usually played again.
